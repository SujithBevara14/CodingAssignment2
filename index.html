Best Fudgy Brownies

Brownies

Recipe by: Sujith Bevara.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup (113 g) unsalted butter
  • 1 cup (200 g) granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup (40 g) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup (64 g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • Optional: 1/2 cup chocolate chips or chopped walnuts

Equipment

  • Medium saucepan or mixing bowl
  • Whisk or spatula
  • 8×8 inch baking pan
  • Measuring cups and spoons
  • Parchment paper (optional)
  • Oven

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Line or grease the pan.
  2. Melt butter. Stir in sugar, then whisk in eggs and vanilla until glossy.
  3. Sift in cocoa, flour, salt, and baking powder; fold just until combined.
  4. Fold in optional chocolate chips or walnuts. Spread batter into the pan.
  5. Bake 20–25 minutes until set at the edges and slightly soft in the center for fudgy texture.
  6. Cool completely before slicing into squares.
